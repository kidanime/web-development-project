So what were going to talk about is league of legends

League of Legends is a multiplayer online battle area game or (MOBA) for short. It was developed and published by Riot Games for Windows and Mac

The game consist of 5 players from both sides.The player of the game assumes the role of a summoner that contols champions with unique abilities. Right now there are 141 champions in league of legends.
